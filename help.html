Getting Started

How does Akazi work?

Akazi enables you to browse the selection of freelancers offering services, and to place orders in just one click. A service offered on Akazi is called a Gig.(Umunyabiraka)

Registration

** Take Action! Click here to join Akazi. **

Once you sign in to Akazi, you will receive an activation email. Just click the link in the activation email to active your account. If you can't find the activation email, check your spam folder.

Note: If your email is already associated with a Akazi account, you will not be able to use it to create a new account.

Accessing My Account

How do I change/reset my password?

Click here to go to your profile settings.

In the “Change Password” section, enter your current password and your new one.

Click “Save Changes”.

My Account Status

Why can’t I access my account?

Have you entered the correct username or email when logging into your account? If you have and you are still unable to access your account, have you tried resetting your password? If not, we recommend doing so first.

If you have already done this and are still unable to access your account, we recommend reaching our to our Support team. They will be able to take a close look into your account to see if there's any restriction preventing you from accessing your account.
